Affiliate Program Terms – AdMultiplier.io

1. Introduction

By joining the AdMultiplier.io Affiliate Program, you agree to promote our platform responsibly in exchange for a 30% recurring commission on customer subscriptions.

This agreement outlines the terms and conditions governing your participation in our affiliate program. By signing up, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to these terms.

2. Eligibility & Enrollment

To join the AdMultiplier.io Affiliate Program, affiliates must:

  • Complete an application and be approved by our team.
  • Maintain ethical marketing practices.

We reserve the right to decline or revoke affiliate status if an affiliate violates these terms or engages in unethical promotional activities.

3. Commission Structure

  • Payout Rate: Earn 30% commission on each payment made by referred customers.
  • Recurring Earnings: As long as your referred customers remain subscribed, you continue
    earning commissions.
  • Payout Schedule:Commissions are paid monthly, provided you meet the minimum payout threshold of $50.
  • Tracking & Attribution: Commissions are tracked using unique affiliate links assigned to
    each partner.

4. Affiliate Responsibilities

Promotional Conduct

Affiliates agree NOT to:

  • ❌ Misrepresent AdMultiplier.io in any way.
  • ❌ Engage in spammy, misleading, or unethical marketing tactics.
  • ❌ Use unauthorized banners, branding, logos, or assets.

Affiliates MAY:

  • ✔️ Use official banners, copy, and promotional materials provided by AdMultiplier.io
  • ✔️ Promote via websites, blogs, social media, email marketing, and paid ads (as long as
    they comply with advertising regulations).

Compliance with Laws

Affiliates must follow all applicable laws and advertising regulations, including:

  • FTC Guidelines for disclosures in affiliate promotions.
  • No false claims, misleading statements, or deceptive marketing.

Failure to comply may result in termination from the program.

5. Termination of Affiliate Status

AdMultiplier.io reserves the right to terminate an affiliate account for:

  • ❌ Policy violations (e.g., unauthorized marketing practices).

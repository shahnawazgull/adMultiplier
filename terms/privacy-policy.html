Privacy Policy – AdMultiplier.io

your data. This Privacy Policy explains:

1. What information we collect

When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and billing details.

We track how you interact with the platform, including:

- IP addresses

3. Data Storage & Processing

Your data is stored securely on cloud-based servers using industry-standard encryption and security protocols.

We NEVER sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

As an AdMultiplier.io user, you have the right to:

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@admultiplier.io.

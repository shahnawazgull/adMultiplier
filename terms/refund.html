Refund Policy – AdMultiplier.io

1. Overview

At AdMultiplier.io, we are committed to delivering high-quality tools that help media buyers, agencies, and ecommerce brands scale ad creative production efficiently. Our Refund Policy
is designed to ensure fair processes for users who feel the platform does not meet their
expectations while preventing misuse of our services.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

To qualify for a refund, customers must meet the following conditions:

    1. Initial Satisfaction Guarantee

    • Refund requests are considered within the first 3 days of subscription.
    • After this period, all sales are final.

    2. Usage Limitations

    • Refunds are only available to customers who have used no more than 1 generated
      variation.
    • If you have created 2 or more variations, you are ineligible for a refund at this level of
      usage indicates significant experience with the platform.

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

1. Submit a Request

  • Contact our support team at support@admultiplier.io.
  • Include:
  • ✅ Your account email
  • ✅ A brief explanation of your refund request
  • ✅ Confirmation that you meet the eligibility criteria

2. Review & Processing

  • Refund requests are reviewed within 7 business days.
  • Approved refunds will be issued to the original payment method (processing time may vary based on your financial institution).

4. Limitations & Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be granted under the following conditions:

  • ❌ Usage Exceeded: Your account has generated 2 or more variations.
  • ❌ Violation of Terms: If your account has misused the platform or breached our Terms of Service.
  • ❌ Late Requests: Refund requests made after the initial 3-day satisfaction guarantee period.
  • ❌ Improper Use of Platform:
  • Attempts to exploit credits, manipulate subscription terms, or abuse the system.
  • Engaging in fraudulent or deceptive activity.

5. Changes to the Refund Policy

AdMultiplier.io reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy. Customers will be notified of any significant changes, and continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
